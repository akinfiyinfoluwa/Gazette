# Exclusive Interview with Burna Boy

In an exclusive interview with Gazette, Grammy award-winning artist Burna Boy talks about his new album, "African Giant 2.0", his creative process, and his plans for the future.

The artist, who has been on a world tour for the past year, revealed that he has been working on the new album for the past two years. "I've been working on this album for a while now, and I'm excited to finally share it with my fans," he said.

Burna Boy also talked about his creative process, revealing that he draws inspiration from his experiences and the world around him. "I'm always writing, I'm always creating. I'm inspired by everything that's happening around me," he said.
